pikachu

Pikachu is an iconic Electric-type Pokémon recognized by its bright yellow fur, long ears with black tips, and a lightning bolt-shaped tail. It stores powerful electricity in the red pouches on its cheeks, which it releases during battle or when threatened. As the most famous Pokémon globally, it symbolizes friendship and incredible electrical potential, often sparking with energy when it senses danger or excitement in the environment.
